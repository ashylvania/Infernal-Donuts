About

Infernal Donuts is an independently owned and family operated business that specializes in the creation of artisan donuts. Established in June of 2006, we have been blown away by the unexpected success and popularity of our supernaturally themed donuts. Our donuts are freshly summoned each morning prior to sun-rise and amongst our most popular donuts include the ectoplasm slime-glazed donut, the full moon pie donut, and our both loved and feared "Mystery Jam of the Week".

Currently we make 33 different types of donuts on a daily basis and have two stores in the San Francisco Bay Area and one store in Los Angeles. Be sure to pop by one of your local haunts to try at least one of our donuts but if you can't make it - don't fret! We have recently introduced both a catering service for those special occassions such as soltices, equinoxes, & birthdays and have also recently introduced a carrier owl delivery service for our "Jump Scare Donut Collection" box which contains different donut flavors both good and bad!
